## 🌏 Who We Are

ARC Gakuen is a Japanese language school with campuses in Tokyo and Kyoto. Since 1986, we have welcomed students from all over the world, providing high-quality Japanese language education combined with deep cultural immersion.

Whether your goal is to enter a Japanese university, build a career in Japan, or simply experience life in a new culture, we are here to support your journey.
